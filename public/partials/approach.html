Our Approach

We embrace the agile ethos for delivering software. From a lean, test-driven approach, where not a single line of code is written without purpose, to tightly honed features, carefully written by listening and understanding you, and your customers.

Our staples include:

  • Test-driven development
  • Weekly delivery
  • Tightly scoped requirements
  • Isolation of functionality
  • A focus on value
  • A focus on customer behaviour
  • Constant interaction with the client
  • Lean Development
