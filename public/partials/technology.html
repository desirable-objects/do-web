Our Technologies

Desirable Objects holds a strong affinity with the open source world. A large number of contributions spanning a variety of open source projects has, and continues to allow us to reap what we sew.

It's true that the correct tool for the job should always be chosen, and often that choice comes down to a few key technologies we consider to be the best options currently available. These technologies include, but are not limited to Grails, Gradle, Groovy, Spock, and Geb.

Production quality tools are no good without production quality platforms, so a variety of Debian based Linux distributions are used on the Server-side as well as the Desktop.
